# Vial Vault — Render Gallery

Fused stepped-honeycomb designs. Render colors are the OpenSCAD preview (teal/blue = walls,
orange = interior) — print in **opaque black/dark filament**. All three are 91 mm / 3.58 in
tall, fit a 220 mm bed, and are fully light-tight (genus 0).
